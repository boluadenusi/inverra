The UEFA Champions League is the premier club football competition in Europe. Founded in 1955 as the European Cup, it was rebranded as the Champions League in 1992.

Top clubs from European leagues compete annually for the trophy. The tournament begins with a group stage followed by knockout rounds culminating in a single final match. Barcelona has won the Champions League five times, with memorable victories in 2006, 2009, 2011, and 2015.

The 2011 final at Wembley Stadium saw Barcelona defeat Manchester United with goals from Pedro, Messi, and Villa. This match is considered one of the greatest Champions League finals ever played.

Real Madrid holds the record for the most Champions League titles with fourteen victories. The competition showcases the highest level of club football and features the world's best players and managers.
