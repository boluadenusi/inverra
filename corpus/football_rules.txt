Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the most popular sport in the world. The game is played between two teams of eleven players each on a rectangular grass field.

The objective is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opposing team's net. Only goalkeepers are allowed to handle the ball with their hands, and only within the penalty area. Matches consist of two halves of forty-five minutes each.

Key rules include offside, which prevents attacking players from waiting near the goal, and fouls, which result in free kicks or penalties. Yellow cards serve as warnings while red cards result in immediate ejection from the match.

Football is governed by FIFA at the international level. The World Cup, held every four years, is the most prestigious tournament in the sport and attracts billions of viewers worldwide.
