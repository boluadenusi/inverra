La Masia is the famous youth academy of FC Barcelona, widely considered the best football academy in the world. The name comes from the traditional Catalan farmhouse that originally housed the facility.

The academy focuses on developing technical skills, tactical intelligence, and the distinctive Barcelona playing philosophy known as tiki-taka. Young players learn to prioritize passing, movement, and ball retention over physical attributes.

Graduates of La Masia include some of the greatest players in football history. Messi, Xavi, Iniesta, Puyol, Busquets, and Pique all came through the academy system. In 2010, the three finalists for the Ballon d'Or were all La Masia graduates.

The academy accepts young players from around the world and provides education alongside football training. Many consider it a model for youth development in professional football.
